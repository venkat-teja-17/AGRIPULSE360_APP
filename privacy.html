Your Ownership of Your Information

We presume you own the information and data that you provide to us when you purchase, set up an account for, access, or use any AgriPulse360 Service Platform product, service, website, or application, including, for example, the data that you transfer or have transferred to us from a third-party account (collectively, "Your Information"). By providing Your Information, you represent that you have the right to share it with us and allow us to use and disclose it as explained in this Privacy Policy.
